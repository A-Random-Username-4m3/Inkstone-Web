FSRS scheduler

Inkstone uses FSRSv6 to schedule the next long-term review. We do not include an in-house optimizer at the moment because of how complex it would be to implement.

Curious what this is? Check out this paper to learn more. It is really interesting and was crazy to me how much thought was put into simply having a specific time when people should study their flashcards.

Developer debug

Override Inkstone's app time for scheduler testing. This affects due queues, FSRS retrievability, list timers, and session expiry, but it does not change browser animation/tap timing.

Backup and restore

Exports settings, enabled lists, imported lists, blacklist, scheduling, and progress to a JSON file. Review logs can also be exported as optimizer-friendly CSV.

FSRS review-log optimization

Review logs are for external FSRS parameter optimization. Export the CSV, run it through a compatible FSRS optimizer, then paste the optimized parameter array into Advanced FSRS parameters above. Don't forget to make a normal backup before changing parameters!

Inkstone reminds you after 400 review-log rows, then again after your stored logs double from the count you last marked optimized.

FSRS optimization reminder

Export your review logs, optimize them externally, then update Advanced FSRS parameters. After you finish, mark this count as optimized.
